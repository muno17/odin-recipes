Basil Fried Rice

Description

Spicy and delicious Thai basily mixture mega ultra combo packed ricey goodness

Ingredients

  • day old rice
  • basil
  • tomato
  • onion
  • green onion
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • all the spices
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • fish sauce
  • egg
  • brocc
  • peas
  • literally anything else you want

Steps

  1. simmer the onion, garlic, ginger, and tomato
  2. add the rice and mix in making sure to use plenty of oil
  3. mix in all the sauces
  4. start adding in all the spices and stir frequently/li>
  5. add in all the veg and stir
  6. make space in the middle and add egg
  7. once egg is cooked, stir it all up again
  8. shovel into mouth
